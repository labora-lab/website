# Welcome to LABORA

This is the first post on the official LABORA blog - Laboratory of Artificial Intelligence, Biomodeling, and Outbreak Response Analysis.

## Our Mission

LABORA is a research laboratory at FGV EMAp dedicated to advancing science in:

- **Artificial Intelligence** applied to Public Health
- **Mathematical Modeling** of Infectious Diseases
- **Epidemiological Data** Analysis
- **Health Surveillance** Systems

## What you'll find here

On this blog, we'll share:

1. Updates on our ongoing research
2. Scientific publications and findings
3. Events and workshops
4. Collaboration opportunities

Stay tuned for more content soon!
