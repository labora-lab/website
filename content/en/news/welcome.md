Welcome to the official website of LABORA - Laboratory of Artificial Intelligence, Biomodeling, and Outbreak Response Analysis!

## Our Mission

LABORA is a research laboratory affiliated with FGV EMAp that combines advanced techniques in artificial intelligence, mathematical modeling, and data analysis to address critical challenges in public health.

## What You'll Find Here

This space was created to share:

- **Research and Publications**: Our scientific articles and research advances
- **Projects in Development**: Tools and systems we're building
- **Analyses and Insights**: Case studies and epidemiological data analyses
- **Events and Collaborations**: Workshops, seminars, and partnership opportunities

## Areas of Expertise

### Artificial Intelligence in Public Health

We develop machine learning and deep learning models for:

- Epidemic outbreak prediction
- AI-assisted diagnosis
- Pattern analysis in large health databases

### Mathematical Modeling

We use mathematical models to:

- Simulate disease transmission dynamics
- Evaluate intervention strategies
- Project epidemiological scenarios

### Data Analysis

We perform statistical and computational analyses to:

- Identify epidemiological trends
- Detect risk factors
- Evaluate the impact of public policies

### Health Surveillance

We develop systems for:

- Real-time monitoring
- Early outbreak detection
- Decision-making support

## Stay Connected

Follow our news and discoveries through this website. We are committed to sharing knowledge and contributing to public health through data science and artificial intelligence.
